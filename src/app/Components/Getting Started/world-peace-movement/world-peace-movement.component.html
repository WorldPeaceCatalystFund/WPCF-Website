WPCF brings 2 technological advancments and new mathematical insight



The Heart Effect - In Icebreakers, you pose a question by selecting a few symbols to represent the question. The application then goes to the interent and retrieves randomly drawn images based off those questions. The application then assembles the images into a feed. WPCF says that there should be a message in that feed of images that answers your question. Science says this should never happen as these images are randomly drawn, however, experimentation is showing otherwise - messages are formed a majority of the time. WPCF is coining this the heart effect - the innate human ability to draw forth what it seeks with their heart. Don't take our word for it, we have experimentation you can do on your own to prove to yourself that you can use Icebreakers to answer your questions.

Reduction of the communication barrier by 90% - Icebreakers is configured so that users remain anonymous to each other. The only thing they know are the non-unique demographic identifiers. This configuration allows for preservation of social currency and enables the free exchange of ideas and values. WPCF has written a report, 'Barriers to Communication' to detail how the communication barrier is reduced by 90%.
