U.N. Sustainability Goal 16.

Promote Peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development



  • In any way to world peace, the peoples of the earth need to understand each other and the only way for this to happen is through cross-cultural communication.
  • Our mission is to catalyze World Peace by facilitating cross-cultural conversations.
  • We provide a systemic social mechanism to determine common grounds (bonds of society). The mechanism revolves around a first of its class, semiotic social media application called Icebreakers(android).

What is Icebreakers?

  • Semiotic Social Media Application - First of It's Kind
  • Reduces communication barrier by 90%
  • Employs the Heart Effect - each conversation is heart based
  • Allow for safe, easy, effective, heart based, connect the dots style, cross-cultural communication



Every cross-cultural conversation on Icebreakers is a heart-thread/brick of peace (however you view it). Each conversation brings us 1 chat closer to world peace. Users send the short conversations in for review and WPCF determines the bonds of society (concpets/thoughts/things/ideas) that bring people together and amplifies them through social media and public policy to bring and maintain a lasting peace.
